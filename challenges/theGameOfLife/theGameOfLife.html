The Game of Life

By John Conway

This is a population game in which represents living and dead communities. This to make it more interesting, this map is like a spherical map, meaning neighbors includes those not directly beside a cell.

It generates a random starting array and then carries out the 3 rules over and over.

  • If a black cell has 3 white cells surrounding it, it becomes white.
  • If a white cell has less than 2 white cells or more than 3 white cells, it becomes black.
  • In all other cases, the state of a cell remains unchanged.
